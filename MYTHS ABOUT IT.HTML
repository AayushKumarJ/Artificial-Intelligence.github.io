MYTHS ABOUT IT


1.AI is going to replace all jobs
     It’s certainly true that the advent of AI and automation has the potential to seriously disrupt labour – and in many situations it is already doing just that. However, seeing this as a straightforward transfer of labour from humans to machines is a vast over-simplification.AI technology as only a method to work in newer and smarter ways.

 2.Only low-skilled and manual workers will be replaced by AI and automation
    This is certainly false. Already, AI-equipped robots and machinery are carrying out work generally reserved for the most highly trained and professional members of society, such as doctors and lawyers.

 3.Artificial intelligence will quickly overtake and outpace human intelligence
     In reality intelligence is measured in many different dimensions. In some of them (for example speed of calculations or capacity for recall) computers already far outpace us, while in others, such as creative ability, emotional intelligence (such as empathy) and strategic thinking, they are still nowhere near and aren’t likely to be any time soon.
